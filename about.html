What the hell is Akella.xyz?

Akella.xyz is a website by me, Alex. I made this to have my own little slice of the internet and to have a page all about me. What does "Akella" mean you may be asking? No idea! It was the name of a Russian software company back in the 90s that dissolved in 2012. I just thought the name was cool and decided to wear it myself. Since the company no longer exists, and Russia is sanctioned to hell right now, I figure I should be fine. (Русский военный корабль, иди нахуй)

To be a cool little hangout! I'm not sure of what else!
